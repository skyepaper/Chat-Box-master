Navigation bar

Navigation bar is always shown. It gives users different options, according to login status.
--- if not logged in- username is 'anon'
--- you have option to LOGIN and REGISTER
--- you have option to post on WALL and see USERS
--- if logged in- username is shown
--- you have option to LOGOUT
--- you have option to post on WALL and see USERS
--- you have access to personal PROFILE page
--- in case there are NEW MESSAGES in the postbox
